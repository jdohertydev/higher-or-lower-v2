Higher or Lower?

Instructions

A simple yet addictive game. What will the next number be? Choose 'higher' or 'lower'.

  • The number will always be between 1–12.
  • It will never be the same number.
  • Use the 'up' and 'down' keys on your keyboard.
  • Watch the screen change colour as you pass through 3 (bronze), 5 (silver) and 10 (gold) streaks.
